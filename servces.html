Orthopedics

Kigali provide high-quality health care in treating trauma (fractures), arthroplasty (joint replacement at a large scale), arthroscopy (joint diseases), spine surgery (cross-linking with neurosurgeons for spine traumatic cases and degenerative diseases), pediatric orthopedics, foot and ankle, bone and soft tissue tumor surgery.
